  • Trousers
  • Jeans
  • Dresses
  • Skirts
  • Double & king size duvets
  • Double & king size sheets

Delivery & Collection

  • Free Pick-up & Drop-off: Within 5 miles of Oswestry.
  • Outside 5 Miles: 45p per mile.
  • Returns: Items returned in the same bag or container unless hangers are provided.

Payment is due upon delivery. Cash or bank transfer (prior to delivery).
